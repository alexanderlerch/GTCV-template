\begin{abstract}
\noindent
Alexander Lerch is an experienced researcher with entrepreneurial background, working in the emerging field of \textit{Music Information Retrieval (MIR)}. MIR is an interdisciplinary research area at the intersection of signal processing, machine learning, music psychology, and systematic musicology. 
Lerch's research focus is on the design of machine learning algorithms for the analysis of audio and music. His research results have contributed new machine learning approaches for music and have led to new insights into music, its performance, and its meaning.\vspace{-1mm}
\end{abstract}
